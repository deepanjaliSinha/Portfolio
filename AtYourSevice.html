AtYourServices

Make your health and life easier and faster

(Personal Project | Google Coursera | UI/UX Designing)

Product and Background

There are many people who needs immediate consultation and medicines which might not be be available in or near there locality. There many localities where there is no doctors or medical store, old people who needs regular check-ups and medicines suffers because of that. In this busy lifestyle we often forget stuffs and most important thing that we forget is our health the unhealthy lifestyle leads to various health issue in later ages. AtYourService focuses on providing accurate, fast, easy and affordable services to the users.

Goals

Design an app that can provide users with medicines and immediate consultation of doctor whenever they need it.

Problems

There are people who lives in remote areas where doctors and medicines are not easily available. For emergency services like sudden need of medicines and consultation of doctor.

Project Scope

App and Web view UI/UX Design

My Role

UI/UX Designer (UX Researcher, Visual Design, Interactions, Animation, Wireframe, Prototype, Usability Testing, Accounting accessibility, Iterating on designs)

Tools Used

Figma, Canva, Unsplash, Slides, Jamboard, Word Doc

Project Duration

8th Mar -12th July, 2022

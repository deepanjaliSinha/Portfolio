Brew Haven Data Dive

Analysis of Company's Sales Performance in April

(Capstone Project | Google Coursera | Data Analytics)

About the Project

Brew Heaven** is a thriving coffee shop, with the potential for significant growth in the upcoming years. Mandy Rich, founder of Brew Heave, believes that analyzing the collected data to date will uncover various growth opportunities for the brand. I have recently joined the company's analytics teams as an assistant analyst and are responsible for examining the sales data to extract valuable insights about the company's products and customers. These insights would be helpful for the company in crafting effective marketing strategies and developing new products that resonate with its target audience, which would lead to brand success.

Problem Statement

How would you rate the performance of Brew Heaven in April? How can the company use its ability to improve customer satisfaction, and strategies to stay competitive in the coffee industry?

Objectives

I tried finding out:

  • Analyses of Company's Sales Performance in April.
  • Analyses of Customer Engangement, Products and Staff Performance in April.
  • Explore and discover insights that contributes improvement of sales and promotion in upcoming months.

Guide and Language Used

  • Guide: Google Data Analytics
  • Tool Used: BigQuery and Tableau
  • Language: SQL and Visualization
  • Industry: Food and Beverages

Data Information

  • Kaggle dataset: Coffee Shop Dataset
  • Data length: 14 attribute and 49,894 rows

Project Duration

12th May - 12th June, 2024

Supermarket Sales Dashboard

Analysis of Data for Supermarket Sales

(Group Project | Spreadsheet | Data Analytics)

About the Project

As part of our college project, we were assigned with a project focusing on "Interpreting large volume of data with various types of graphs and drawing inferences" based on this topic, we opted to develop a comprehensive sales dashboard, providing users with an overview of company sales and product lines.
The supermarket industry, characterized by rapid growth and occasional challenges, but offers ample opportunities for innovation. Beyond products, the customer experience and ambiance significantly influence sales. Through data analysis, we aimed to uncover insights beyond sales figures, including the best-selling products, locations with the highest customer visits, and customer perspectives on the brand. This valuable information assists the company in making informed decisions for future reforms and development.
In this project we have used supermarket sales dataset from Kaggle, which has recorded data about the product sales in three different branches of supermarket company in Myanmar (Burma).

Objectives

Throughout the project our objective was to:

  • Build a dashboard the gives the overview about the different product sold in branches.
  • Find out which branch is doing highest sales.
  • Which product line is not getting much of the customer attention.
  • Overall sales of the company across differenct braches, product line and customer type.

SelfStyle

Empower Your Wardrobe: Where Fashion Meets Confidence

(Personal Project | Female Fashion Brand | UI/UX Designing)

Background

SelfStyle emerges from a deep understanding that confidence is at the core of authentic self-expression. With a brand name synonymous with individuality and empowerment, SelfStyle is not just a shopping app, it's a celebration of your unique journey, a nod to your strength, and an ode to your unparalleled style.
Guided by the ethos that confidence is the most beautiful outfit you can wear, SelfStyle aspires to redefine the digital fashion landscape. The project is rooted in the belief that every woman deserves to feel powerful, confident, and beautiful, irrespective of societal norms. Welcome to SelfStyle, where your confidence meets its most fashionable expression.

Goals

SelfStyle's aspires to revolutionize the female clothing app by infusing it with interactive, adaptive, and innovative features. These enhancements, in alignment with the brand values, seek to precisely meet user needs in a way that exceeds expectations, offering a uniquely satisfying and progressive experience.

Problems

This app aims to address the problem by serving as a singular destination where users can effortlessly discover clothing that complements their style, create, share, and explore new combinations, providing a comprehensive solution to the limitations observed in other apps.

Project Scope

Mobile view UI/UX Design

My Role

UI/UX Designer (Visual Design, Interactions, Animation, Wireframe, Prototype, Iterating on designs)

Tools Used

Figma, Canva, Unsplash

Project Duration

28th Aug -27th Dec, 2022

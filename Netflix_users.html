Netflix Userbase Analysis

Analysis Userbase of Netflix in between 2022-23

(Personal Project | Python Programming | Data Analytics)

About the Project

Netflix stands out as a flourishing OTT platform, boasting millions of subscribers globally. The rapidly expanding OTT industry has abundant opportunities for leveraging innovative Machine Learning/AI techniques, particularly in the realm of customer management. Effective customer relationship management, tailored subscription offers, adaptive platform interfaces, and strategic partnerships are crucial for retaining users, who are critical in driving the revenue of such services. Netflix's diverse content library, is a result of strong collaborations with various production houses, that offers an extensive range of series and movies to its audience.
As a loyal user of Netflix for an extended period, my interest in the platform has prompted me to dive into the analysis of its user dataset. For this project, I utilized the Netflix user dataset available on Kaggle, focusing on exploring and analyzing user behavior between 2022-23. This exploration aims to uncover trends and insights that contribute to a deeper understanding of user preferences and interactions with the platform during this timeframe.

Objectives

I tried finding out:

  • The subscription plan with the highest sales through analysis.
  • Analysing user behaviour and trends between 2022-23
  • Explore and discover insights that contributes to user preferences
  • Estimating potential relationships between various attributes within the user dataset.

Guide and Language Used

  • Guide: Alex The Analyst
  • Tool Used: Google Colab
  • Language: Python Programming
  • Industry: Entertainment and Media

Data Information

  • Kaggle dataset: Neflix Userdataset
  • Data length: 10 attribute and 2500 rows

Project Duration

9th Nov - 11th Nov, 2023

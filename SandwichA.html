SandwichA

An end-to-end Mobile app for Sandwich Lovers

(Personal Project | Google Coursera | UI/UX Designing)

Product and Background

I've designed an application SandwichA, tailored for a sandwich shop, with a primary user base of working adults and senior citizens. The inspiration behind this app stems from the challenges faced by the shop, such as overcrowding and chaos, resulting in customers leaving unattended or with cold food. This situation has led to customer dissatisfaction and a decline in overall patronage. The app aims to offer a seamless and efficient solution, ensuring customers receive their orders promptly and warm, while also helping the shop streamline its operations and reduce chaos.

Goals

  • To build an app which can provide faster and easier way to order and receive Sandwich.
  • To build an organized way to attend more and more customers.

Problems

  • The customer has to stand in long line and wait before they place their order.
  • The place is overcrowded and unorganized that many times customers don't get a place to sit and eat, and they leave the shop unattended.
  • Placing group orders is difficult.

Project Scope

Mobile view UI/UX Design

My Role

UI/UX Designer (UX Researcher, Visual Design, Interactions, Animation, Wireframe, Prototype, Usability Testing, Accounting accessibility, Iterating on designs)

Resources Used

Figma, Canva, Unsplash, Slides, Jamboard, Word Doc

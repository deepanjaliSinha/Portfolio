Evolution of Movies Over the Years

Analysis of Trends in Movie Industry

(Academic Project | Team Project | Data Analytics)

About the Project

The world of cinema is a captivating realm, filled with stories, emotions, and cultural influences. But behind entertainment lies a complex industry driven by audience preferences, production decisions, genres, and box office revenue. To analyze the trends of movies over the years, we chose the "TMDB Movies" dataset from the Kaggle website for this case study. This dataset offers a treasure trove of information about various movies. It contains details like genres, budgets, release dates, popularity, and more. By analyzing this data, we can explore fascinating trends, identify audience preferences, and gain insights into the recent trends in the film industry.

This case study covers the details about the dataset, the cleaning and preprocessing of the data, the categories and measures we chose, and the analysis and visualization of the data. Through these visual representations, we uncovered hidden patterns and trends within the world of movies.

Problem Statement

How has trends in movie industry changed with time. Are there seasonal trends? Do certain genres gain or lose popularity over time?

Objectives

I tried finding out:

  • Analyses of trends in Genre over the years.
  • Analyses of factors that impacts the success/failure of the movies.
  • Explore and discover insights to suggest strategies and plans to improve customer engagement and revenue generation.

Guide and Language Used

  • Team Details: Group of 3 students
  • Guide: Tableau Guide and Our Course Professor
  • Tool Used: Tableau and Google Colab
  • Language: Python and Visualization
  • Industry: Entertainment Industry

Data Information

  • Kaggle dataset: TMDB Movies
  • Data length: 25 attribute and 9,06,373 rows

Project Duration

25th Jan - 30th March, 2024
